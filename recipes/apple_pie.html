Apple Pie

Description

  • Unbleached all-purpose flour
  • Granulated sugar and brown sugar
  • Salt
  • Unsalted butter
  • Vanilla extract
  • Apples
  • Lemon
  • Cinnamon and nutmeg
  1. Prepare and blind bake pie crust
  2. Make the crumb topping
  3. make the apple pie filling
  4. Assemble pie
  5. Bake
  6. Let cool to warm

The reason why dutch apple pie is better than regular apple pie is because of the delicious crumb topping which gives it an extra crunch.
