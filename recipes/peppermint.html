Peppermint Milkshake

Description

  • milk
  • vanilla ice cream
  • peppermint extract
  • crushed peeprmint candies
  • sweetened whipped cream
  1. In a blender, combine milk, ice cream and extract
  2. cover and process for 30 seconds or until smooth
  3. Stir in 1/4 cup peppermint candies
  4. Pour into chilled glasses
  5. Top with whipped cream, remaining peppermint candies and cherry

A favorite beverage for the holidays is none other than a peppermint milkshake; available at local coffee shops, most fast-food chains, or buy the ingredients in local market to make them from scratch.
